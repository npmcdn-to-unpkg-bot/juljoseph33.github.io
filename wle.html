Women's Leadership Event

Core Team Designer

I created all design assets for the annual intern Women's Leadership Event at Johnson & Johnson in the summer of 2016.

The Event

Every year, the Healthcare Technology team at Johnson & Johnson runs an intern Women's Leadership Event, with the goal of connecting and empowering female interns across the enterprise. Earlier in the summer, I applied and was accepted to join the Core Team for the WLE in the role of Designer. For this event, I designed and created the event logo, badge design, and agenda (shown below). Everything was made using Photoshop.
