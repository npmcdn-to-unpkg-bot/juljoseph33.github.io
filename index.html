Who I Am: The Basics

I am a junior at Cornell University, studying Information Science, Systems, and Technology at Cornell's College of Engineering. I grew up in Rye Brook, New York, located in Westchester County.
On campus, I am:

  • a member of the Big Red Marching Band Clarinet Section

In my spare time I like to read, make playlists on Spotify, run, make lists, and listen to poetry on YouTube.
