DailyUI Challenge

Personal Project

I worked on the DailyUI Challenge during the spring and summer of 2016, in order to improve my User Interface Design and Visual Design skills.

The Challenge

I signed up for the DailyUI challenge because the only way for me to better my design skills is to practice! Every weekday I was emailed a new UI design challenge. I would do a quick sketch of my design idea and then create it in Sketch. I set aside an hour every day to do the challenge, and I am currently 76 days in. For more information about the challenge, please go to http://www.dailyui.co/. You can find all of my designs on Twitter (https://twitter.com/JuliaRJoseph) with the hashtag #dailyui.
